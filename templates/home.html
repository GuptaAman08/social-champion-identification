Topic Sensitive Influence

We leverage the concept of homophily in social networks to estimate how connected every follower is to the candidate. The consolidated influence of every follower is determined by the volume tweets of the other friends of the follower as well as the similarity of the candidate and follower's tweets

Magnitude of Engagement

Measures the engagement of every follower with the candidate through the likes and retweets of every tweet. This metric serves as a proxy check on the apparent follower-based influence. Likes measure user affinity with the tweet as well as the twitterer. Retweets measure the content value of the tweet and indicates its diffusion through the network.

Topic Relevance

We measure the relevance of the candidate's tweets to the areas of interest of the NGO.
